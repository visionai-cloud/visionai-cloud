## 👋 Intro

I'm an **AI / Machine Learning Engineer** focused on building and shipping LLM-powered products — from retrieval-augmented systems and multi-agent workflows to voice agents and end-to-end automation pipelines.

My work spans the full lifecycle: designing agentic architectures with **LangChain** and **LangGraph**, fine-tuning and serving models via **Hugging Face**, building **RAG** systems over private data, and wiring it all together with automation platforms like **n8n**, **Make**, **Zapier**, and **Power Automate**.

I care about clean data pipelines, reliable evaluation, and getting models out of notebooks and into production.

> _Feel free to explore my projects and connect if you'd like to collaborate!_
